# Privacy Policy for ElevatEd TechAI Toolbox

**Last Updated:** January 18, 2025

## Introduction

ElevatEd TechAI Toolbox ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Diagnostic Information**: Vehicle diagnostic data, VIN numbers, and repair information you input into the app
- **Account Information**: Email address and subscription details when you subscribe to premium features
- **User Inputs**: Text, images, and other data you provide when using AI-powered diagnostic tools

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, and app version
- **Usage Data**: Features accessed, session duration, and interaction patterns
- **Camera Access**: Photos taken within the app for diagnostic purposes (VIN scanning, wiring diagrams, waveform analysis, MSDS labels)
- **Location Data**: Approximate location for waste disposal facility recommendations (only when using Waste Disposal Guide feature)

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide AI-powered automotive diagnostic assistance
- Process vehicle identification numbers (VINs) and provide vehicle specifications
- Analyze diagnostic trouble codes (DTCs) and provide repair recommendations
- Process images for wiring diagram analysis, waveform interpretation, and VIN scanning
- Manage your subscription and provide premium features
- Improve app functionality and user experience
- Provide safety information (MSDS lookup and waste disposal guidance)

## Third-Party Services

### Google AI (Gemini)
We use Google's Gemini AI API to process:
- Diagnostic questions and vehicle symptoms
- VIN decoding and vehicle information lookup
- Image analysis for wiring diagrams, oscilloscope waveforms, and VIN plates
- Safety data sheet (MSDS) information
- Chemical waste disposal guidance

Data sent to Google AI is processed according to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### Google Play Billing
Subscription payments are processed through Google Play Billing. We do not store your payment information. Google's billing system handles all payment processing according to [Google Play's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage**: Most diagnostic data is processed locally on your device
- **AI Processing**: Data sent to AI services is processed in real-time and not permanently stored by us
- **Encryption**: All data transmitted to third-party services uses industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS)
- **No Sale of Data**: We do not sell your personal information to third parties

## Camera and Photo Permissions

The app requests camera access for the following features:
- **VIN Scanner**: Capture VIN plate photos for AI-powered VIN extraction
- **Wiring Diagram Navigator**: Photo wiring diagrams for AI analysis
- **Waveform Analyzer**: Capture oscilloscope waveforms for diagnostic interpretation
- **Safety & Compliance**: Scan product labels for MSDS information

Photos are:
- Processed immediately and not permanently stored on our servers
- Sent to Google AI for analysis only when you explicitly use these features
- Not shared with any other third parties
- Not used for any purpose other than the specific diagnostic feature you're using

## Location Permissions

Location access is requested only for:
- **Waste Disposal Guide**: Finding nearby chemical waste disposal facilities
- **Weather Data**: Providing local environmental conditions for HVAC diagnostics

Location data is:
- Only accessed when you explicitly use location-dependent features
- Not tracked or stored permanently
- Not shared with third parties except weather and mapping services when necessary

## Data Retention

- **Session Data**: Diagnostic session data is stored locally on your device only
- **Account Data**: Subscription information is retained for the duration of your subscription
- **AI Queries**: Processed in real-time through Google AI and not retained by us

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request information about data we have collected
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your account and associated data
- **Opt-Out**: Disable camera and location permissions in device settings
- **Data Portability**: Export your locally stored diagnostic data

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes through the app or via email for subscribed users.

## Developer Mode

The app includes a hidden "Developer Mode" accessed by tapping the logo 7 times. This mode enables premium features for testing purposes only and does not collect additional data.
