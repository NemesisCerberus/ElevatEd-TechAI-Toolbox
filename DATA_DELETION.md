# Data Deletion Request - ElevatEd TechAI Toolbox

**Last Updated:** January 18, 2025

## How to Delete Your Data

ElevatEd TechAI Toolbox stores minimal data locally on your device. You can delete your data using the following methods:

---

## Method 1: Uninstall the App (Complete Data Deletion)

The easiest way to delete all app data:

1. Go to your Android device **Settings**
2. Navigate to **Apps** → **ElevatEd TechAI Toolbox**
3. Tap **"Uninstall"**
4. All locally stored diagnostic data will be permanently deleted

---

## Method 2: Clear App Data (Keep App Installed)

To delete data while keeping the app:

1. Go to Android **Settings** → **Apps** → **ElevatEd TechAI Toolbox**
2. Tap **"Storage"**
3. Tap **"Clear Data"** or **"Clear Storage"**
4. All locally stored diagnostic sessions will be deleted

---

## Method 3: Request Account Data Deletion

If you have a premium subscription and want to request deletion of any associated account data:

**Email us at:** info@elevatedautosolutions.com

**Include in your email:**
- **Subject line:** "Data Deletion Request"
- Your Google Play account email (used for subscription)
- Confirmation that you want to delete all associated data

**We will process your request within 30 days.**

---

## What Data is Deleted

When you delete app data, the following is removed:

- ✅ Locally stored diagnostic sessions
- ✅ VIN decoding history
- ✅ User preferences and settings
- ✅ All cached data

---

## What Data is NOT Stored

Our app does NOT permanently store:

- ❌ Photos taken with the camera (processed in real-time only)
- ❌ AI diagnostic queries (processed by Google AI ephemerally)
- ❌ Personal information on our servers

---

## Subscription Data

Subscriptions are managed by **Google Play Billing**. To cancel your subscription:

1. Open **Google Play Store**
2. Tap **Menu** → **Subscriptions**
3. Select **ElevatEd TechAI Toolbox**
4. Tap **"Cancel Subscription"**

Note: Canceling your subscription does not delete app data. Use Method 1 or 2 above to delete local data.

---

## Data Retention Period

- **Local data:** Stored indefinitely on your device until you manually delete it
- **AI processing data:** Not retained (processed ephemerally by Google AI)
- **Subscription data:** Managed by Google Play according to their retention policies
